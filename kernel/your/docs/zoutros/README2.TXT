
--------------------------------------
Building:
    Type 'make'

--------------------------------------
Running:
    Go into the ama/ folder and type './run' or './runnokvm'

--------------------------------------
Configuration file:
    include/beauty/config.h

--------------------------------------
The boot loader starts at:
    boot/vd/fat/main.asm

--------------------------------------
The kernel starts at:
    kernel/arch/x86_64/startup/head_64.asm.
